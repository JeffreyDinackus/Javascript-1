This website has a bunch of graphs on it, and the first one is the most important. It shows the difference between what the businesses are making off of labor vs how much they are paying back to their employees.

If you don't know how to read graphs you need to work on that as a lot of truth comes in graph form. Try searching on YouTube how to do it.

Career in Tech

Freecodecamp Youtube Page

This is a good way to learn the basics of a lot of different stuff for free from pretty good instructors. They also have a website where you can earn a certificate for free which mostly focuses on web development technologies and data science. The YouTube page has a wide variety.

Udemy.com

This is a really good website to get courses on tech especially but other things like music too. If you want to pursue a CompTIA certification you can find some good series for a modest price such as the ones by James Dion. I have only done his course on Linux+ I also like Stephane Maarek's AWS courses but you have to get the practical experience on your own.

Wait until courses go on sale for 10-20 dollars. Do not buy for full price.
